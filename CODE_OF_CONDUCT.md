# Code of Conduct — transcribe‑assistant

The transcribe‑assistant project is an open‑source initiative built to provide safe, ethical and accessible real‑time transcription tools.  
To maintain a healthy, supportive and professional community, all participants — users, contributors and maintainers — are expected to follow this Code of Conduct.

This document is based on the Contributor Covenant version 2.1.

---

## 1. Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- age  
- disability  
- ethnicity  
- gender identity or expression  
- level of experience  
- nationality  
- physical appearance  
- race  
- religion  
- sexual orientation  

We aim to create an environment where everyone feels **welcome, respected and safe**.

---

## 2. Our Standards

### Positive behavior includes:

- showing empathy and kindness  
- respecting different viewpoints and experiences  
- giving and receiving constructive feedback  
- taking responsibility for mistakes  
- focusing on what benefits the community  

### Unacceptable behavior includes:

- offensive comments or personal attacks  
- harassment, threats or intimidation  
- publishing others’ private information  
- disruptive or aggressive conduct  
- any actions that compromise privacy or safety  

---

## 3. Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- defining and enforcing community standards  
- moderating discussions, issues and pull requests  
- taking action when violations occur  
- removing content or contributions that violate this Code  

Maintainers have both the right and the obligation to **protect the project and its community**.

---

## 4. Scope

This Code applies to all project spaces, including:

- the GitHub repository  
- Issues and Pull Requests  
- discussions and comments  
- private communication related to the project  

---

## 5. Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code, please report it to:

**krunixbase@gmail.com**  


All reports will be handled seriously, confidentially and respectfully.

---

## 6. Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction  
**Consequence:** Private, informal reminder of expected behavior.

### 2. Warning  
**Consequence:** Formal warning with explanation of the violation.

### 3. Temporary Ban  
**Consequence:** Temporary restriction from participation.

### 4. Permanent Ban  
**Consequence:** Permanent removal from the community.
